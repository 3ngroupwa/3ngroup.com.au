Our Story

Your Trusted Perth Tradesmen

3N Group is a Perth-based painting, renovation and property maintenance company built on a simple promise: quality workmanship you can trust.

We started with a focus on professional painting services and quickly expanded to meet our customers' needs - kitchen renovations, hybrid and laminate flooring, and comprehensive property maintenance.

Today, we're proud to be one of Perth's most reliable trade services, serving homeowners, investors, and businesses across the metropolitan area.

Our motto is simple: Every job, no matter how big or small, gets our full attention and commitment to excellence.
